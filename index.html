FAQ

How will this concert be different from a theatre experience? A live orchestra concert can have variations in volume and balance and allows the audience to feel the music, creating a more engaging experience. A concert can be more special and memorable than the theatre, creating a sense of occasion.
Are cameras and recording devices allowed in the theatre? Sorry, but cameras and recording devices are not allowed in the theatre. We have a strict policy of respecting the privacy and enjoyment of all audience members.
Do I need to watch Ratatouille before attending? You can attend a live orchestra performance of Ratatouille without having seen the movie beforehand. It is not necessary to watch the movie prior to attending the concert.
Is there a dress code for orchestra concerts? In general, formal or semi-formal attire is commonly expected, but this can vary depending on the venue and the type of concert.
How old do you need to be to attend the concert? Typically, all ages are welcome to attend the concert. Some may recommend that younger children be accompanied by an adult due to the sound intensity.
